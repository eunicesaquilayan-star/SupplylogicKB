Terms, Use, & Definitions
  • Acknowledgement / Vendor Confirmation / Order Acknowledgement
    Confirmation of order from a vendor confirming product specifications, pricing, and estimated ship date. The AS must look for discrepancies between the confirmation and the SLWM PO.
  • AP ‚Äì Accounts Payable
    Accounting term describing money SLWM owes, usually to a vendor.
  • AR ‚Äì Accounts Receivable / Collections
    Accounting term describing money clients owe SLWM.
  • AS ‚Äì Account Specialist
    Employee that assists clients with marketing needs.
    Old Titles: MAS, CSS, CSR
  • ASI ‚Äì Advertising Specialty Institute
    Largest member organization for the promotional products industry. SLWM is registered as an ASI distributor.
  • Barracuda
    Spam filter and email archiver found on the SLWM Toolbox.
  • BO / B/O ‚Äì Backorder
    Occurs when a client orders an item that is out of stock in SLWM‚Äôs warehouse.
  • Benchmarking
    Evaluating performance by comparison with a standard.
  • BAS ‚Äì Bill As Shipped (SLWM Owned Inventory)
    Inventory stored for a customer that has not yet been paid for. Items are billed as released.
  • BM ‚Äì Brand Manager (SupplyLogic)
  • Client
    Businesses SLWM provides marketing services for. Clients are relationship-driven; customers are transaction-driven.
  • Client Dashboard
    Includes Order Search, Finance, Toolkit, Sourcing, and Reporting.
  • Concur
    Expense, travel, and invoice management system. Vendor discrepancies are reviewed here.
  • Connect
    SLWM‚Äôs asset management system for housing art files.
  • Cost
    What SLWM pays vendors before selling to clients.
  • Customer Owned Inventory
    Inventory paid for upfront by the customer and stored in SLWM or offsite warehouses.
  • DemandBridge (DB / DbD)
    Ordering, inventory, reporting, and accounting system.
  • Vendor Discrepancy
    Vendor invoice does not match PO or inventory not received.
  • Division Code
    Identifies which SLWM division a client account belongs to.
  • ESP ‚Äì ASI Store
    Used to search promotional products and supplier information.
  • GPM ‚Äì Gross Profit Margin
    Percentage markup over cost.
  • Inventory / Stock
    Products produced and stored for fulfillment.
  • JEM ‚Äì Job Entry Management
  • JIRA
    Internal project and support request management tool.
  • KPI ‚Äì Key Performance Indicator
    A measurable value used to track performance over time.
  • MarketingBench (MB)
    Client online ordering site built by SLWM.
  • Markup
    Difference between sell price and cost.
  • PayCom
    SLWM payroll and timesheet system.
  • Purchase Order (PO)
    Official document authorizing vendors to produce goods.
  • POD ‚Äì Print On Demand
    Items produced as orders are placed and not inventoried.
  • Promo / Promotional Products
    Branded giveaway items such as pens, apparel, and accessories.
  • Proposal / Quote / RFQ / RFP
    Request or pricing document for goods or services.
  • RTF ‚Äì Real Time Freight
    Freight setup for MarketingBench credit card orders.
  • Sell Price
    Price billed to the client (always higher than cost).
  • SLA ‚Äì Service Level Agreement
    Defines service expectations and penalties.
  • Slack
    Internal communication tool for SLWM teams.
  • SourcingBench (DB/a)
    Web-based procurement solution for quotes and POs.
  • SOP ‚Äì Standard Operating Procedure
    Step-by-step instructions for consistent operations.
  • SOW ‚Äì Statement of Work
    Legally binding document defining project scope.
  • Microsoft Teams
    Internal communication tool for Account Specialists.
  • Toolbox
    Internal SLWM portal with system links.
  • UOM ‚Äì Unit of Measure
    How a product is sold or released (Each, Box, Pack).
  • Vendor / Supplier
    Partners that produce print and promotional products.
  • WM / WMM ‚Äì SLWM Marketing
    Integrated portfolio of marketing solutions.
  • WMC ‚Äì SLWM Commercial
    SLWM Marketing‚Äôs sister print production company.
  • Back-ordered
    Product that is temporarily out of stock but still available for purchase. The seller has ordered more from the supplier and expects to fulfill the order later.
  • Call Drivers
    Common reasons for customer contact including account access, order placement, order status, and tracking inquiries.
  • Check
    A written payment instruction from a bank account holder.
  • Credit Card
    An electronic payment method allowing immediate payment using a bank-issued card.
  • Demand Bridge
    Tool where order-related information is stored.
  • Dialpad
    Primary calling and communication platform. AI-powered system for inbound calls and customer interaction.
  • Impersonate
    Ability to access a customer‚Äôs account to place an order on their behalf when requested.
  • MarcomCentral
    Brand management and distributed marketing platform for storing and managing official materials.
  • MyMarketingBench
    Central platform used to access marketing insights and assets to support customer discussions.
  • Net Terms
    Payment terms on an invoice that define the payment deadline.
  • Pending Status
    Indicates an order has been placed but has not yet shipped.
  • Purchase Order (PO)
    Official document issued by a company authorizing a vendor to provide goods or services.
  • Shopify
    Platform allowing visibility into customer profiles and recent orders during calls.
  • SupplyLogic
    End-to-end sourcing and fulfillment solution for multi-location brands. Provides visibility into spend and ensures brand consistency.
  • User Budget
    Pre-approved spending limit assigned to a specific user or department.
