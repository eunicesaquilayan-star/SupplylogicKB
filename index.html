Terms, Use, & Definitions
Term
Description
Acknowledgement / Vendor Confirmation / Order Acknowledgement
Vendor confirmation of product specifications, pricing, and estimated ship date. Account Specialists must check for discrepancies against the SLWM Purchase Order.
AP – Accounts Payable
Accounting term describing money SLWM owes, usually to vendors.
AR – Accounts Receivable / Collections
Accounting term describing money clients owe SLWM.
AS – Account Specialist
Employee that assists SLWM clients with marketing needs.
Old titles: MAS, CSS, CSR
ASI – Advertising Specialty Institute
Largest member organization in the promotional products industry. SLWM is a registered ASI distributor.
Back-ordered
Product temporarily out of stock but available for purchase and expected to ship later.
BO / B/O – Backorder
Occurs when a client places an order for inventory that is out of stock.
Barracuda
Spam filter and email archiving tool found in the SLWM Toolbox.
Benchmarking
Evaluating performance by comparing it to an established standard.
BAS – Bill As Shipped
SLWM-owned inventory stored for customers that has not yet been paid for. Items are billed as released.
BM – Brand Manager
SupplyLogic role responsible for brand execution and strategy.
Call Drivers
Reasons customers contact SLWM including account access, order placement, order status, and tracking.
Check
Written payment instruction issued from a bank account holder.
Client
Businesses SLWM provides marketing services for. Clients are relationship-driven; customers are transaction-driven.
Client Dashboard
Portal containing order search, finance, sourcing, and reporting tools.
Concur
Expense, travel, and invoice management system used for vendor discrepancies.
Connect
SLWM asset management system for storing customer artwork.
Cost
Amount SLWM pays vendors for products before selling to clients.
Credit Card
Electronic payment method allowing immediate transaction processing.
Customer Owned Inventory
Inventory paid for upfront by customers and stored in SLWM or offsite warehouses.
DemandBridge (DB / DbD)
SLWM ordering, inventory, reporting, and accounting system.
Dialpad
Primary calling and AI-powered communication platform.
Division Code
Identifies which SLWM division a client account belongs to.
ESP – ASI Store
ASI distributor platform for sourcing promotional products.
GPM – Gross Profit Margin
Percentage markup over cost used to calculate selling price.
Impersonate
Accessing a customer account to place orders on their behalf.
Inventory / Stock
Products produced and stored for future fulfillment.
JIRA
Internal project and support request management system.
MarketingBench (MB)
Client-facing online ordering site built by SLWM.
Net Terms
Invoice payment terms defining when payment is due.
Pending Status
Indicates an order has been placed but not yet shipped.
Print on Demand
Orderes can be placed by a client through their MarketingBench Site or by an AS through DemandBridge or SourcingBench. They are orders shipping to a location other than a SLWM or Offsite Warhouse.Items NOT Inventoried - Items produced by a vendor as orders are placed
Real Time Freight
RTF needs to be set up for items that are ordered through the MB site as credit card orders.
SupplyLogic
End-to-end sourcing and fulfillment solution for multi-location brands.
User Budget
Pre-approved spending limit assigned to a user or department.
Unit of Measure
How is the product is sold/release to a customer. Examples: Each, Box, Pack, Carton, Pad.
Unit of Measure
The ability to change information on a printed product or to select a color, size or logo for an apparell or promo item

Marcom Central Function

ACCOUNT ACCESS

1. Login to the MarcomPortal using your credentials. Website: https://admin.marcomcentral.app.pti.com/

2. Click the FusionPro Central logo to ensure you are at the home screen screen to make sure you are at the home screen of the portal.

By using this portal, you can access the list of customers that we have listed on the portal. Remember that you can only access the portal by using VPN.

Changing to the Caller’s Account

1. Make sure you are at the HOME SCREEN. Click the dropdown to choose the caller’s account.

2. Click the dropdown button on this part of the screen to choose the account of the caller.

It will show you the list of the accounts and URLs of customers that we have on our portal like the image below.

3. Choose the account of the caller to start accessing their account. (e.g. Buff City Soap)

4. Click on PORTAL USERS.

5. Select USERS to see the users under the Customer’s account.

After clicking the USERS, this will take you to the image below. Please take note that “YOU WILL ONLY ACCESS THIS AREA.”. You will not access any other page inside the Portal.

On this part of the portal, you can search the users by User’s Name, external ID, and user ID. The User’s name format is First Name Last Name. You can also search them by a part of their name in case the user only provided it. An example image is seen below.

Changing User Account Details (Changing Password)

1. Search the account of the user by their provided details. (e.g. Burke.)

2. Double Click on the name of the correct account of the user. (Remember not to click the Login Button, but on the name of the User’s Account)

3. It will take you to this screen. .

4. Click EDIT on the top right to make changes.

Click EDIT on the top right to make changes.

5. Change the password of the user on Password Text Box. Make sure to put the same Password on the Confirm Password Text Box.

6. Inform the caller about the password change and they need to change the password to their preferred password once they are able to login back to their account.

7. Click the save button on the top right of the screen.

Change the password and confirm in the Confirm Password box. Click save.

We have products that are custom made like business cards. You can search it using the search box above. Below is an example on how it will look like.

They need to fill up the details of the item first. Once we are able to fill in the details for the item. Click PREVIEW before you check out. You can’t proceed if you will not click PREVIEW.

ORDER CHECKOUT

Once you are done with the items you want to add in your cart. Click CART on the top menu to view all of your items.

This will take you to this screen.

You can also remove items on your cart by clicking the TRASH BIN icon before the Product name.

Once you are done reviewing the items, you can click on the CHECKOUT on the bottom right part of the screen.

SHIPPING

Once you click the CHECKOUT button, it will take you to the shipping details screen where you need to input all the shipping details.

Our customer’s store addresses are mostly saved in their accounts. It will auto populate on the EXISTING ADDRESS. If they want to send it to a different address, you need to input it on the USE A NEW ADDRESS (THIS ORDER ONLY).

Choose the SHIPPING METHOD on the bottom left part of the screen.

Choose the right shipping method according to the preference of the customer then click CONTINUE on the bottom part of the screen.

BILLING

On this page, you need to put the BILLING DETAILS of the customer. Most of the customer’s billing information is already added to our system.

The process is almost identical to the SHIPPING. However, we need to choose the PAYMENT METHOD according to the customer’s preference.

Once all of the information is provided, click on CONTINUE to finalize reviewing the order. It will take you to this screen.

Always remember to review the items with the customer and their preferred payment method, billing address and the total amount that will be charged before completing the order.

MarketingBench Function

Changing Password on MYMARKETBENCH (Legacy: DBE)

1. Use the customer’s URL and log in to your account on MyMarketingBench using your login credentials.

2. Once logged in. It will take you to this screen.

3. Click on the WORK WITH USERS on the left side pane.

4. Search the name, User ID, First Name or Last name of the caller. (e.g. Pattison)

5. Select the right account then click EDIT on the right side of the screen to start making changes.

6. Put a temporary password. Make sure to put the same Password on the Confirm Password Text Box.

Inform the caller about the password change and they need to change it once they are able to login back to their account.

ORDER INQUIRIES

This will tackle how to navigate orders on mymarketbench(DBE).

1. Open the client’s business portal with their corresponding URLs and credentials.

1. Once you are logged in, click HOME on the top menu.

2. Click on my MY ORDERS to see the orders inside the client’s portal.

3. Search for the order of the customer by their order #, or filter the date when it was placed. You can filter the list on this part of the screen.

4. Once you found the order, click on the ORDER # on the first column to get more information about the said order.

Your screen will show something similar to the image below. You can click the tracking number below to take you to more detailed information of the shipment of their order.

DemandBridge Function

HOW TO LOG IN

1. Once the log in page load type your log in ID and hit enter *note if you type anyting it's not visible on your end but system capture.

2. If ID is correct, the option to put your password will show, again it's not visible when you type just hit enter.

3. Once log in successfully wait for the next prompt and choose number 2 (DB GUI).

4. Wait for the prompt and then click accept

5. Another prompt will show, please click continue.

6. Folders will show, please double click DB Distributor.

7. Then under Distributor click Accounts Receivable.

8. Then choose setp up.

9. Then Customer folder.

HOW TO SEARCH ORDER

1. Click ORDER LOOK UP tab

2. Type the order number, then click Order history by item.

3. Once this prompt show click proceed.

4. This screen will show close and open orders, Orders that are highlighted in green are still open. Double click the order number

5. Then it will route you to the order details
